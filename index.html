About

Ridgeway Ghost is a melodic metalcore project named after the shapeshifting phantom that haunts the backroads between Blue Mounds and Dodgeville, Wisconsin. It changes forms at will. For example, a large black dog, a headless horseman, demented animals, incorporeal figures, etc.

Ridgeway Ghost is built as a side-project. Maybe I'll dedicate more to it in the future, but for now it's just a hobby.

The Ghost

The band name comes from Wisconsin folklore. The Ridgeway Ghost is a phantom said to haunt an old mining road and later the stretch of highway (18/151) between Dodgeville and Blue Mounds (Ridgeway and surrounding area). People claimed it appeared as a man with a whip, a headless horseman, animals in the fields, and strange figures by the side of the road.

Oral history/stories say the haunting began after a killing near a local saloon in the 1800s, and that sightings spike every few decades.
